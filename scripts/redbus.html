Bangalore to Hyderabad Bus Overview

The Bangalore to Hyderabad bus route covers approximately 567 kms and takes about 9 hrs 29 mins. A total of 375 direct buses operated by 116 bus operators run on this route. The first bus departs at 00:20, and the last bus leaves at 23:59. 

Major boarding points in Bangalore include Agara, Anand Rao Circle, Attibele, BTM Layout, Bagepalli, Baiyappanahalli, Banashankari, Banaswadi, Bannerghatta Road, Battarahalli, while common drop locations in Hyderabad are A S Rao Nagar, Abids, Afzalgunj, Allwyn colony x road, Alwal, Amberpet, Ameerpet, Aramghar, Ashok Nagar, Attapur.

RTC and private operators such as Orange Tours And Travels, Northern Travels, IntrCity SmartBus, Jabbar Travels, National travels offer Volvo, AC, and non-AC seater and sleeper buses. Ticket prices on redBus range from INR 600 - INR 9999.00 depending on the bus type and features.
